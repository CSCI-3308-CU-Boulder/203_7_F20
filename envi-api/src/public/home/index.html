Welcome to envi!



Recycle ~ Conserve ~ Track ~ Connect

envi is an environmental impact tracking website that encourages users to practice environmentally friendly activities such as using reusable water bottles and straws (save the turtles!), donating clothes, biking instead of driving, and many more! Users can connect with friends and share their progress and achievements as well as donate to organizations. Sign up to save our planet!
